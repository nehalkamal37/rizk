Welcome to Psychologist & Family Consulting


My mission is to provide a compassionate, evidence-based approach to support your mental health journey.

I am a licensed clinical psychologist, committed to providing high-quality psychotherapy and diagnostic assessment. I specialize in health psychology, where I deeply value the connection between physical and mental health. No matter the struggle you're facing, I will walk alongside you during your journey towards wellness.

Now, more than ever, mental health should be a priority. To improve access to care, I offer secure video telehealth services only — ensuring comfort, privacy, and flexibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some common questions to help you feel more prepared and comfortable reaching out.

What can I expect in the first session?

We'll talk about your concerns and goals, and I'll ask questions to better understand your history and needs. Together, we’ll create a plan that works for you.

Do you accept insurance?

I’m an out-of-network provider. You’ll pay directly and may request a superbill to seek reimbursement from your insurance company.

How do I pay for sessions?

I accept cash, Venmo, PayPal, and credit cards. Credit cards incur a 3% processing fee. Payment is due at time of service.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes, all sessions are conducted via secure telehealth video for your convenience and safety.

Insurance & Payment

I’m committed to making mental health care accessible, transparent, and flexible. Here's how insurance and payment work in my practice.

Insurance

I am an out-of-network provider, meaning clients pay directly. You may still be eligible for reimbursement through your insurance. I provide a detailed Superbill upon request, which you can submit to your insurer. I can also guide you on what to ask your provider about mental health benefits.

Payment
